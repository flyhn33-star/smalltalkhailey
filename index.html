🌟 핵심 표현 연습
💡 영화 대사를 소리 내어 읽어봐요! 한국어 뜻을 보고 영어로 말해보세요.
💡 영화 대사를 더 쉬운 영어로 바꿔서 말해봐요!
Original
"I miss her in every way."
그녀가 정말 그리워요
Try saying it like this ✨
→ "I really miss her." / "I think about her all the time."
Original
"I realized the key to this whole deal was to keep moving."
가장 중요한 건 그냥 몸을 움직이는 거였어요
Try saying it like this ✨
→ "I learned that I need to stay active." / "The most important thing is to keep busy."
Original
"There's a hole in my life, and I need to fill it."
내 인생에 어딘가 빈 구석이 있고 그걸 채우고 싶어요
Try saying it like this ✨
→ "I feel like something is missing." / "I want to do something meaningful."
Original
"Don't get me wrong, I'm not an unhappy person."
오해하진 마세요 난 불행한 사람은 아니에요
Try saying it like this ✨
→ "Please don't misunderstand me." / "I am actually a happy person."
